Per Medjat
House of Written Knowledge

The public interface for ontology-aligned collections of the Iroko Historical Society. Records are presented with explicit stewardship boundaries; governed material is signaled, not silently erased.

1 collection live · 50 pilot records · 750+ plant corpus in development · 3 collections planned

pr mdꜣt / Per Medjat, house of books, house of scrolls (Ancient Egyptian). The institutional form of the Medjat: a governed house of records where access, transmission, and stewardship are made explicit. Cf. Yorùbá Ilé, the house that holds the tradition.
