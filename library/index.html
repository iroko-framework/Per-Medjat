Published holdings · Cataloged materials

Medjat Library

Scholarly and reference materials supporting research into Afro-Atlantic sacred knowledge systems. Published works, field editions, ethnographies, and reference literature held and cataloged by the Iroko Historical Society. Materials in the Medjat Library are published works; they are maintained separately from the unique and unpublished materials held in the Medjat Archives.

IHS Research Library
Scholarly monographs, ethnographies, field studies, and comparative literature on Lucumí, Vodou, Candomblé, Palo Monte, Abakuá, Ifá, and related Afro-Atlantic traditions. Acquired to support archival description, contextual research, and ethical stewardship of the Society's archival collections.

Distinction from the Medjat Archives

The Medjat Library holds published and cataloged materials. The Medjat Archives holds unique, unpublished, and field-collected materials governed under the Iroko Framework six-tier access system. A donated collection may include both published works (cataloged here) and unique field materials (governed in the Archives). The distinction is maintained throughout processing and description.
